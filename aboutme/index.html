Jo

I live in Windhoek Namibia. I was born in Budapest, Hungary and lived there with my mother for the first 6 years of my life. Magyar is the first language I spoke. Later after moving to Africa, I forgot all of magyar and learned English and my african tribes' language. I am not fluent in either one of them.

I worked for the World Food Programme as a finance officer, as well as for the Global Fund as a Finance Manager. In these positions I managed a lot of money and lead lots of people. I however felt I needed to take care of my children more, and left my work eight years ago.

With all these years of staying at home, I became an expert in cooking, changing diapers, motivating others, teaching, giving emotional support, being a good example, collaboration, team work, playing at the play park,driving, negotiating, being peacemaker and or mediator and running up and down with doctors and hospitals at odd hours. Now I just want to do web development.
